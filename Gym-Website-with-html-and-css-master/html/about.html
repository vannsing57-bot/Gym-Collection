Our Story

Two products. One standard.

Forgewear started in a garage gym with a chalk bucket and a spreadsheet. We got tired of shirts that shrank and supplements that hid their dosages, so we started making both ourselves.

The Apparel

Cut for training, not for racks.

Every shirt is pattern-tested through squats, pull-ups, and overhead presses before it's approved. We pre-shrink the fabric so the fit you try on is the fit you keep.

Heavyweight cotton was chosen on purpose — it holds its shape wash after wash and doesn't go see-through under sweat.

The Creatine

One ingredient, dosed straight.

No proprietary blends, no filler ingredients padding the label. Every batch is micronized for mixability and sent to an independent lab before it ships.

5 grams a scoop, every scoop, every tub — that's the whole formula.
